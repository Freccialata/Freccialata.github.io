3D toy robot

July 2017

This is a character made for a videogame in VR. In the game you control a kid who wakes up in his room and is attaked by his own toys and he has to defend himself with a plastic gun. One of the toys is the robot toy you see here.

The main references for this model were the old plastic toy robots for kids, but my team and I decided to give him a metallic look with damaged details of bad industrial paint and many scratches.
